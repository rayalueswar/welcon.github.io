Our Business Philosophy

  • Since its inception in 1985, Welcon has
    blossomed into a leading water treatment
    system provider

  • Especially for the beverage industry in India.

  • Services include design,
    supply erection commissioning of water treatment
    plant (WTP) tailored to suit specific
    needs of our client

  • We have wide customer base
    ranging from beverages, sugar industry,
    entertainment, engineering, hotels,
    government sector etc

  • Specifically tapered into beverage
    segment in the last 15 years even more exciting.




Our Expertise

  • Continuous coagulation system (as well as batch treatment)

  • Basic pressure vessels (Pressure sand Filter,
    Activated Carbon Filter, Pesticide Removal Filters)

  • Water Softening (Zeolite Based) System

  • De-Mineralization System
    (SAC, WBA + polishing mixed bed filters)

  • Ultra Filtration System Reverse Osmosis Units
    (used in packaged drinking water segment)

  • Automatic Mineral Injection System
    (used in packaged drinking water segment)

  • Dosing Systems (pH control, chlorine dosing etc)

  • Water Recovery Systems (to minimize water footprint of WTP)

  • De-Alkalizers for carbonated soft drink manufacture.
